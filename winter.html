Winter

Winter can be the brutalist season in Montana. It gets very cold, most of the time the temperature doesn't get over 40 degrees. Montana is also one of those unique states that some towns experience winter differently. For example towns on the highline get way colder winters than Helena. The most wonderful time of year is also during winter... Christmas! New years is also during winter time.
