The Effects of Coffee

Coffee enters the bloodstream fairly quickly which means it reaches the brain quickly as well. It is said that caffeine takes about 10 minutes to completely reach the brain. Even though drinking coffee can bring people benefits like alertness, helping them stay awake, it can also has side effects. Too much caffine can cause insomnia, and give people the jitters. Too much caffine can also cause an over stimulization of the brain which can lead to confusion
