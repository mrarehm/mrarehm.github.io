Fall

Fall is one of the nicest seasons in Montana. It usually is a good moderate temperature. The temperature is usually around 40 degrees. There are two major holidays in fall: halloween, and thanksgiving. Fall is also one of the best seasons in Montana, because it is usually pretty nice. Fall can be the busiest time of year. There are numerous activities happening all the time. There are corn mazes, pumpkin patches, haunted houses, and football games.
