Summer

Summer in my opinion is the best season in Montana. The temperatures are in the high 70s and 80s. Montana also has a lot of water access. There are numerous lakes and resevoirs, and a lot of rivers all over the state. Fishing is great here, people come from all over to fish. Flathead lake is probably the most well known lake in Montana, it is extremely popular. The summer months also have one of the best holidays... the 4th of July!

The best season.
